Homemade Lasagna

Description

This easy, homemade lasagna will change your life...seriously!

Get the keys to your car. Go to any store and purchase Stouffer's frozen lasagna. Why do you care? Because it takes 70min to cook and you just have to pop that bad boy in the oven. I get it, "homemade" is relative.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Lasagna
  • Ground beef
  • Crushed tomatoes

Steps

  1. Get in car
  2. Drive to store
  3. Buy frozen Stouffer's lasagna
  4. Throw in the oven
  5. BOOM, delicious!
